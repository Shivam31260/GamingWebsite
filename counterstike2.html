Counter-Strike 2 - Specifications

Minimum System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-7500 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti or AMD Radeon RX 560
  • Storage: 30GB available space

Recommended System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-9700K or AMD Ryzen 7 3700X
  • Memory: 16GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 or AMD Radeon RX 5700 XT
  • Storage: 30GB available space

Additional Features:

Counter-Strike 2 offers a revamped experience with improved graphics, enhanced audio, and new gameplay features while maintaining the competitive core of the Counter-Strike franchise. It is designed to take full advantage of modern hardware capabilities.
