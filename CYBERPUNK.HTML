Cyberpunk 2077 - Specifications

Minimum System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 7/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K @ 3.4GHz or AMD FX-8310
  • Memory: 8GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 780 3GB or AMD Radeon RX 470
  • Storage: 70GB available space (SSD recommended)
  • DirectX: Version 12

Recommended System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-4790 @ 3.6GHz or AMD Ryzen 3 3200G
  • Memory: 12GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB or AMD Radeon R9 Fury
  • Storage: 70GB available space (SSD recommended)
  • DirectX: Version 12

Additional Features:

Cyberpunk 2077 offers a highly detailed open-world set in the futuristic city of Night City. With deep customization options, ray tracing capabilities, and immersive storytelling, it sets a benchmark for RPGs.
