Call of Duty - Specifications

Minimum System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K or AMD Ryzen R5 1600X
  • Memory: 8GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 2GB or AMD Radeon RX 470 4GB
  • Storage: 175GB available space

Recommended System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-4770K or AMD Ryzen R5 1600X
  • Memory: 12GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB or AMD Radeon RX 580 8GB
  • Storage: 175GB available space

Additional Features:

Call of Duty offers an intense first-person shooter experience with fast-paced gameplay, stunning visuals, and a variety of multiplayer and single-player modes. It features breathtaking graphics and has strong multiplayer support with seasonal updates and in-game events.
