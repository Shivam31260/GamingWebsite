The Witcher 3 - Specifications

Minimum System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 7 64-bit or Windows 8 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K 3.3GHz or AMD Phenom II X4 940
  • Memory: 6GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7870
  • Storage: 35GB available space

Recommended System Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7 3770 3.4GHz or AMD Ryzen 3 2200G
  • Memory: 8GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB or AMD Radeon RX 580 8GB
  • Storage: 35GB available space

Additional Features:

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt offers an immersive, open-world role-playing experience with stunning visuals, a deep narrative, and hours of gameplay. The game is known for its breathtaking landscapes, intense combat, and a richly detailed world full of characters and lore.
